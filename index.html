    • About me

    I am a rising senior at UC Berkeley, pursuing simultaneous degrees in political science and environmental economics and policy, and a minor in journalism. I have a strong passion for social and environmental justice issues and have experience working in various settings, from teaching at a special-needs school in Tanzania to providing legal assistance for low-income clients at San Francisco Superior Court, as well as researching environmental policy with the Wilson Center in D.C. In my free time, I love traveling, hiking, and photography. I climbed Mt. Kilimanjaro, the tallest mountain in Africa, when I was 16 years old.

    Photography is how I express myself...
